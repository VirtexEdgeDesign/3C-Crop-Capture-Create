Use Imgur to Host Image?

Capture Crop Create(3C) uses Imgur to host your image when you share it to other platforms like Twitter, Facebook, Pinterest etc...


The next page will ask you to link your Imgur account to 3C. If you don't have an Imgur account, you can create one using your Twitter, Facebook or Google Account on the next page.


If you don't want to use Imgur, click 'go back' below, save the image to your hard drive and share it manually from your platform of your choice!



Note: Capture Crop Create (3C) and Virtex Edge Design are not affliated with Imgur.
